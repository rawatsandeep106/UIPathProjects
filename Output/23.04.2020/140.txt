<div class="subject">SUBJECT:&nbsp;&nbsp;Questions and Answers Related to Interagency 
   Guidance on Authentication in an Internet Banking Environment</div>
<p>To assist the banking industry and examiners, the Board of Governors of the 
   Federal Reserve System and the other Federal Financial Institutions Examination 
   Council (FFIEC) agencies have issued the attached frequently asked questions&nbsp;(FAQs) 
   related to the FFIEC guidance on <i>Authentication in an Internet Banking 
   Environment</i>. The FAQs are designed to assist financial institutions and 
   their technology service providers in conforming to the guidance, by addressing 
   common questions on the scope of the guidance, risk assessments, timing, and 
   other issues. </p>
<p>Reserve Banks are asked to distribute this SR&nbsp;Letter and the attached 
   FAQs to the banking organizations supervised by the Federal Reserve, as well 
   as to their supervisory and examination staff. If you have any questions regarding 
   the FAQs, please contact Stacy&nbsp;Coleman, Assistant Director, Operational 
   and IT&nbsp;Risk Section, at (202)&nbsp;452-2934, Elton&nbsp;Hill, Senior 
   Supervisory Financial Analyst, at (202)&nbsp;452-2514, or John&nbsp;Gibbons, 
   Supervisory Financial Analyst, at (202)&nbsp;452-6409. </p>
<div class="officer">William G. Spaniel<br>
   Acting Director</div>
